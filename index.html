Stock History

Problem

Investors and Managers sometimes need insights in how companies are doing and how sections of the markt are doing. When they won't have insights in companies and stockprices they would take a huge risk.

Response

Underneath the tab History we see three different visualisations. When the page is unloaded for the first time we see information about the company Microsoft. We see a line chart with information about the stock prices form the past 100 days. Also we can see an interactive barchart with the volatility (riskyness) of the stocks. Furthermore there is a bubble chart which contains all the companies that are within the S&P500 stock market index.

This brings us to the reason why the company Microsoft is displayed when you click on the tab History. Microsoft is the company with the heighest weight wighin the S&P500 and has because of that reason the most influence on the index.

S&P 500

In short the S&P 500 stock market index is an index just like the Dow Jones index or the Nasdaq index. It is a measurement of a section of the stockmarket. Investors and financial managers use them to describe the market and compare returns on investments.

The S&P 500 Index, started in 1957, is a stock market index of 500 large publicly traded American stocks. The stocks in this index are from all sectors of the economy and are selected by a committee. To be selected, stocks must have a market cap of $8.2 billion or more (as of 2019), have a public float of at least 50 percent, have positive earnings for the most recent four quarters, and have adequate liquidity as measured by price and volume.

User Guide

On the page History you can see a searchbar. In this search bar you can enter Ticker Symbols from any company. For instance Apple: AAPL, Google: GOOG, FaceBook: FB. If you do this and hit enter you can see that the line chart will be updated and so will the barchart with the volatilites. Another way to retrieve information about a company is to click on the bubble chart and you will see the line chart and barchart updating.

Data Collection

But how is the data from all the companies retrieved? That is done using an API key. Which you can get at alphaadvantage.co. Every time the user makes a request, the data will be collected and the graphs will be updated updated. Because of that we have real time information about the stockprices and we can use this website in half a year and it will be up to date.
